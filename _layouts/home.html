About Publications CV

Xingdi (Eric) Yuan

Senior Researcher · Microsoft Research, Montréal

I aim to develop machines that can read, write, and use language as a tool, in both static datasets and interactive worlds.

Current Focus

My recent work focuses on teaching coding AI to better understand codebases and to debug using tools. Find our recent work in Coding AI on the Froggy team's page .
